## 4. FAQ SECTION (Schema-Ready)

### Question 1
**Q**: Are your PDF tools really free?  
**A**: Yes! All our PDF tools are 100% free to use with no hidden costs, no registration required, and no watermarks on your files. You can convert, merge, split, and edit unlimited PDFs.

### Question 2
**Q**: Is it safe to upload my PDF files?  
**A**: Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All files are encrypted during upload and automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour. We never store, share, or access your documents.

### Question 3
**Q**: Do I need to install any software?  
**A**: No installation needed! All our PDF tools work directly in your web browser. Simply upload your file, process it online, and download the result. Works on Windows, Mac, Linux, and mobile devices.

### Question 4
**Q**: What file formats do you support?  
**A**: We support all standard PDF files. For Word to PDF conversion, we accept DOCX, DOC, and RTF formats. All tools maintain the original quality and formatting of your documents.

### Question 5
**Q**: How do I add page numbers to my PDF?  
**A**: Upload your PDF to our Add Page Numbers tool, choose your preferred position (header, footer, or corners), select the number format, and click 'Add Page Numbers'. Your numbered PDF will be ready to download in seconds.

### Question 6
**Q**: Is there a file size limit?  
**A**: We support large files up to 100MB. This is enough for most documents, ebooks, reports, and presentations. For best performance, we recommend files under 50MB.

### Question 7
**Q**: Can I use these tools on my phone?  
**A**: Yes! Our PDF tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on mobile phones and tablets. Simply visit our website from any device with a web browser.

### Question 8
**Q**: How long does PDF processing take?  
**A**: Most operations complete within 5-15 seconds depending on file size. Simple tasks like rotation or adding page numbers are nearly instant, while merging large files may take a bit longer.
